THE RTR STORY

The TVS Apache RTR series is a popular line of motorcycles manufactured by TVS Motor Company, an Indian two-wheeler manufacturer. The "RTR" stands for "Racing Throttle Response," highlighting the sporty and performance-oriented nature of these bikes. The Apache RTR series has gained a reputation for its innovative features, sporty design, and impressive performance, making it a favorite among motorcycle enthusiasts.

The Apache RTR bikes are known for their aggressive and sporty design, featuring sharp lines, aerodynamic fairings, and distinctive styling elements. The design language is often inspired by the world of racing.RTR series is designed for performance enthusiasts, with a focus on quick acceleration, nimble handling, and responsive throttle. Many models are equipped with advanced features like race-tuned suspension, dual-channel ABS (Anti-lock Braking System), and high-performance tires.

TVS has incorporated various technological innovations into the Apache RTR series. Some models feature Bluetooth connectivity, allowing riders to pair their smartphones with the bike's instrument cluster for navigation, call alerts, and more. Additionally, the bikes may have ride modes, adjustable suspension, and other advanced features.RTR series has gained a strong following among motorcycle enthusiasts in India and other countries where TVS motorcycles are available. The bikes are known for their balance of performance, features, and value for money.
